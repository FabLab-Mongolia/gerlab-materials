# Machine–Material Compatibility

[← Previous: Material Approval](material-approval.md) · [↑ Materials Library](README.md) · [Next: Laser Materials →](laser-materials.md)

This matrix identifies common candidates, not automatic approval.

| Process | Common candidates | Conditional examples | Never assume |
|---|---|---|---|
| CO₂ laser | Known laser-grade wood, paper, cardboard, acrylic, approved textiles | Coated/painted stock, rubber, foam, engineering plastics | Unknown plastic, PVC/vinyl, metal cutting capability |
| CNC router | Plywood, MDF, timber, machinable sheet plastic, nonferrous metal when approved | Composite panels, acrylic, aluminum | Hidden fasteners, unknown laminate, hazardous dust material |
| CNC mill | Aluminum and other verified machinable metals/plastics | Steel, stainless, engineering plastic | Correct tool, coolant, workholding, and parameters without review |
| Desktop mill | Wax, machinable plastic, PCB material, soft metal within machine limits | FR materials, composites, coated boards | Dust/fume safety or machine capacity |
| FDM printing | PLA, PETG, approved TPU | ABS/ASA, nylon, PC, PP, filled filament | Nozzle/bed compatibility, ventilation, dryness, food safety |
| SLA printing | Printer-compatible Formlabs resin | Specialty, engineering, castable, dental/biocompatible resin | Cross-printer compatibility or safe skin contact without full validated post-cure |
| Vinyl cutting | Approved self-adhesive vinyl, stencil film, transfer media | Reflective, flock, thick or abrasive media | Laser compatibility—vinyl cutter approval does not mean laser safe |
| Vinyl printing | Printer-approved media and ink system | Laminate, specialty film, textiles | Ink adhesion, outgassing, durability, heater compatibility |
| Vacuum forming | HIPS, ABS, PETG and approved thermoplastic sheet | Acrylic, polycarbonate, specialty sheet | Correct forming temperature, mold release, food/contact status |
| Metal saws | Verified metal stock within capacity | Stainless, hardened stock, nonferrous alloys | Blade/wheel suitability or safe workholding |

Use [Material Approval](material-approval.md) for any new combination.
